About

Hi! I'm Francis Wu, a husband, dad, and product designer based in Montreal. I've had the good fortune of working with some amazing people who taught me so much. Now I try to pay it forward. I'm currently a product designer at Negosh.

I started tinkering with design at the age of 10 when my family got an Apple IIc and a copy of The Print Shop. What followed was a love affair with PCs, bootlegged copies of Photoshop, and a degree in computer science.

Eventually I learned HTML and CSS, and joined half a dozen startups as founding designer doing front-end development.I've seen what "good" looks like and I'm committed to helping others recognize they need not settle for less.
