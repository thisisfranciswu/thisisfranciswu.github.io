About

Hi! I'm Francis Wu, a husband, dad, and product designer based in Montreal. I've had the good fortune of working with some amazing people who taught me so much. Now I try to pay it forward. I'm currently a product designer at Negosh.

I started tinkering with design at the age of 10 when my family got an Apple IIc and a copy of The Print Shop. What followed was a long love affair with PCs and bootlegged copies of Photoshop that culminated in a degree in computer science.

Joining a B2B internet startup in '98 revealed customer issues obviously rooted in poor UI design. I thought this was something I could help people with. I read The Design of Everyday Things and Don't Make Me Think. I learned HTML and CSS, and grew into what we'd call today a Front-of-the-Front-End Web Developer or UX Engineer .

Since then, I've worked with test-driven developers and data-informed product managers. I eventually became a product manager myself, leading a great team that deployed code multitple times a day. I've seen what "good" looks like and I'm committed to helping others recognize they need not settle for less.
